Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out?
Check-in starts at 3 PM, and check-out is at 11 AM.
Do you offer airport transportation?
Yes, we offer a complimentary shuttle service to and from the airport. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance to schedule.
Is breakfast included in the room rate?
Breakfast is included with select room packages. Please check your booking details or ask the front desk upon arrival.
Are pets allowed?
Yes, we are a pet-friendly hotel. There is a small cleaning fee of $25 per pet per stay.
What amenities are available?
We offer a spa, sauna, two restaurants, a swimming pool, and a fitness center for our guests.
What is your cancellation policy?
Cancellations are free up to 24 hours before your scheduled arrival. Late cancellations will incur a one-night stay charge.
