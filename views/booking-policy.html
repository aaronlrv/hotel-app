Booking Policy

At StayEase, we strive to ensure your stay is seamless and enjoyable. Please review the following policies before making a booking:

Booking Confirmation

  • Bookings are confirmed upon receipt of full or partial payment as required.
  • A confirmation email will be sent to the email address provided during booking.

Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellations are allowed up to 48 hours before the check-in date.
  • Cancellations made within 48 hours of check-in will incur a charge equivalent to one night's stay.
  • No-shows will be charged in full for the booking.

Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in time: 10:00 AM
  • Check-out time: 10:00 AM
  • Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and additional charges.

Additional Guests

A charge of $50 per extra adult and $10 per extra child (above 2) will apply.
