Our Amenities

Swimming Pool

Our luxurious outdoor swimming pool is the perfect place to unwind, recharge, and enjoy the beautiful surroundings. The pool is surrounded by lush greenery and comfortable lounge chairs, creating a serene oasis where you can escape the everyday hustle. Whether you prefer a refreshing swim in the morning or a relaxing dip in the evening, our pool offers a calming retreat for all our guests.

Timings: Open daily from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM

Relaxing Sauna

Our state-of-the-art sauna is designed to provide the ultimate relaxation experience. With its modern design and tranquil ambiance, the sauna helps you rejuvenate your mind and body. The gentle warmth not only soothes tired muscles but also promotes circulation and reduces stress.

Timings: Open daily from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM
